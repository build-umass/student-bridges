Web Series

The pilot program ‘Bridges’ web series (launched in Spring of 2018) was created to give the opportunity to further promote the mission of the agency and the Soul TV Registered Student Organization by engaging one on one with underrepresented student populations and providing them a chance for their voices to be heard. It is important for these voices to come light because marginalized groups are often silenced and left invisible. This will provide a platform to encourage, engage, and inspire all underrepresented students to express themselves and tell their stories. This offers more diverse representation on media platforms; enabling motivation for members of alike groups which can contribute to an increase in retention rates here on the campus of UMass Amherst.
