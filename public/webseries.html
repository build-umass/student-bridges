Web Series

The ‘Bridges’ web series was created to give the opportunity to further promote the mission of the agency and the Soul TV RSO by engaging one on one with underrepresented student populations and providing them a chance for their voices to be heard. It is important for these voices to come light because marginalized groups are often silenced and left invisible. This will provide a platform to encourage, engage, and inspire all underrepresented students to express themselves and tell their stories.
