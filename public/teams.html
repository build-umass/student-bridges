Our Team

Yedalis Ruíz yruiz@umass.edu

Director

Yedalis is the director, course instructor and faculty advisor for Student Bridges. She earned a Ph.D. at UMass, in the department of Educational Policy and Research with a concentration in Higher Education. Yedalis graduated with a Bachelors of Arts in Psychology and Education from Mount Holyoke College and earned a Master’s degree in Policies Studies in Education from the College of Education at UMass. Yedalis is a first-generation, bilingual Puerto Rican college graduate dedicated to addressing issues of access and equity in higher education. Through her professional and academic work, she has focused on the critical analysis of the educational trajectories of minoritized students. Her dissertation research examined the high school student college-going identity formation process to expand existing theories of college access and choice to include the experiences, knowledge and beliefs of youth of color who are first generation college aspirants.

Olga Correa sbgpc-sa@umass.edu

Graduate Program Director

Olga is a second year PhD student in the Educational Policy and Leadership Program at UMass. She earned her Master’s in Higher Education from UMass in 2014 and a Bachelor’s in Sociology from William Paterson University in 2012. Olga identifies as Latina, specifically Colombian and Venezuelan, and grew up in a Spanish-speaking household in NYC and NJ. Olga’s research and career interests focus on expanding the availability of school-based mental health supports for underrepresented students in K-12. Trauma influences student learning and achievement, therefore the implementation of supports to support specific student’s needs will increase high school graduation rates along with college acceptance, retention, and completion rates. This fuels her passion and desire to continue working with youth - they are our future!
