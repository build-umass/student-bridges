About Us

Student Bridges is a non-profit, student run agency that works to improve access and success for underrepresented students.

Learn more

Our Mission

Our mission is to increase college access and success for underrepresented students on the university campus and in our neighboring communities by building partnerships at UMass, and with local schools and community organizations. We also offer college awareness, preparation and success activities, and advocate for enhanced institutional and public policies and practices.
