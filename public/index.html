About Us

We are a student-initiated outreach program that connects UMass students with local community-based organizations and schools through:
  • Tutoring-mentoring partnerships
  • College awareness activities
  • Policy advocacy

Our Mission

Our is to increase college access and success for underrepresented students on the university campus and in our neighboring communities by:
  • Building partnerships at UMass, with local schools and community organizations
  • Offering college awareness, preparation and success activities
  • Advocating for enhanced institutional and public policies and practices.
